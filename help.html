Mastering the Comment System

Posting a Comment: Navigate to the Comments section of any article or post. Click on the "Post Comment" button to open the comment box. Type your thoughts and insights, then click "Post Comment" to share your comment with the community.


Replies: To respond to a specific comment, click on the "Reply" button located beneath the comment. This opens a nested comment box where you can address the author directly or continue the discussion with others.



Likes: Show appreciation for a comment by clicking the "Like" button next to it. Likes indicate agreement or appreciation for the comment's content and contribute to the visibility of helpful posts.

Dislikes: If you believe a comment is unhelpful or off-topic, you can express your disagreement by clicking the "Dislike" button. Dislikes help moderate the quality of discussions and keep the platform focused on constructive dialogue.
